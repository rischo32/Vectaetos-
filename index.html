This is not a system.

It is a configuration of tension.

It does not interpret.
It does not conclude.
It does not authorize.

Speed erodes coherence.

Qualitative Epistemic Aporia is not collapse.
It is the visible edge of answerability.

Truth is not assumed.
Uncertainty is represented.

What appears here is projection.

Projection is generation without authority.
Without recommendation.
Without verdict.

Enter projection
