What Vectaetos Is

Vectaetos is not an agent, not an oracle, not a decision system. It is an epistemic field designed to maintain coherence under uncertainty.

  • No prescriptions
  • No decisions
  • No optimization goals
  • No moral enforcement

Core Principles

  • Φ as primary ontology
  • 4 Epistemic States + QE Aporia
  • Coherence as predicate, not objective
  • Impulse as unrealizable transition
  • Runes as projection, never command
  • NIR — Non-Intervention Regime

Pipeline

Human Input
 → Epistemic Gate
 → Field Φ
 → Coherence Predicate K(Φ)
 → Runic Projection
 → LLM (language adapter)
 → Descriptive Output

NIR is active across the entire pipeline.
